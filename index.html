Our Mission

AVCF strives to better the lives of Prayas Children. We want the best for our students in the hope that they will have prominent futures and will become productive members of society.

Help us in our mission to improve these children's lives. They deserve our help however small it might be it will go a long way in helping these young kids. the Indian government does not offer mentally challenged students admission into state run schools and so we hope to provide a means for education and another chance for them.
